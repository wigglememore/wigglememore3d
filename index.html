Welcome to my website where I will document my journey through 3D printing

My name is Matt and I'm a CFD engineer by trade, but maker by night. I recently (depending on how often I update this website) purchased a Creality Ender 5 Pro, and decided to document my journey through building, calibrating and modding in a simple website hosted here on GitHub.

The aim of this website is to help guide anyone using this printer by recording all of the failures and successes I have, and how I deal with issues that arise along the way.
